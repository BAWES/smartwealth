Activate your account

In order to activate your account, we need you to fill
out some details in order to generate the contractual
agreements for you to sign digitally.

Upload ID

In order to verify your identity, we kindly ask you to
upload a copy of your Civil ID and a secondary ID.
You can upload the file on next page

Sign Documents

On the next page you can view and sign the legal and
regulatory documents required to open your first
portfolio. Copies of the agreements will be sent to you
email directly, once signed.

To edit any information on the documents; click on the
Complete Profile step and select what you wish to edit
and re-launch the documents again.

8min 1 Complete Profile

  • Personal Information
  • Contact Information
  • Employment & Wealth
  • Taxes & Beneficiaries

2min 2 Upload ID

For a verification purpose we need a digital copy of your:
  • Civil ID
  • Passport or Kuwait Driver's License

5min 3 Sign Documents

Your documents will be pre-filled with your personal information and ready for you to
sign digitally in just a few minutes.
